You can sign up for the Free Plan with just an email - no credit card needed. It gives you basic filters to explore leads in 1 country. Click "Get Started" on our pricing section to begin!

The Free Plan lets you explore leads with 15 people credits/week and basic filters, but no exports. Starter ($249/mo) adds 1,000 people credits/mo, 15 product credits/mo, and lets you export people lists. Both cover 1 country.

You can export people lists with Starter, Growth, and Enterprise Plans. Only Enterprise lets you export product lists up to your custom limit. Free Plan does not support exports.

Free Plan credits expire weekly, Starter credits expire monthly. Growth and Enterprise plan credit rollover policies will be announced soon.

Growth and Enterprise Plans are launching soon! They will offer more filters, credits, and countries. Join the waitlist on our pricing section to get notified.

We are here to help! Paid plans do not offer cash refunds, as noted in our terms of use, but if you spot incorrect product or people data, just let us know. We will review it and add credits back to your account within 24 hours.
